Hello World!
It’s time to share our story.

  • Nikhil Raikwar
  • May 22, 2021

NikhilTech was founded in 2021 with a vision to let millions of people around the globe unlock the power of Internet. We began by giving away fresh content towards coding and programming for free. I mean it, absolutely for free. For everyone. Almost unlimited. We are bootstrapped, growing, profitable, & proud!

We’re programmers, gamers, hackers, runners, developers, customer support agents, DevOps analysts, readers, travelers, volunteers, parents and hard workers. We can be serious, without taking ourselves too seriously. We have the courage to spark change – to make our products and technology, our people and our place better.

Our vision is to enable millions of people around the globe to unleash the power of Internet. To give them the empowerment to learn, create and grow. We help our customers to succeed online. It’s a big undertaking. We have learned a lot already and expecting to discover even more. Here we’ll be writing about problems we face, tools we use and things we learn.

If you’re just browsing, enjoy your stay. If you’d like to become a customer, we’d love to have you.
